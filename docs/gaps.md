create a what-if page where users can simulate different investment scenarios and see potential returns.

example - 
I decided to invest Rs 100000 in weekly recommendations starting 01-Jan-2025. I put 50% in top recommendation, 30% in second and 20% in 3rd. I purchase monday morning and sell on friday evening before close of business. 
The next week, my investable amount is the total sum of money from the previous week. I repeat this process for 52 weeks. If I gain, my investment amount increases, if I lose, my investment amount decreases.
compare this with investing the entire amount 100000 in niftybees for 52 weeks. I want to compare the returns of both strategies.
All of these variables - initial amount of investment, investment strategy (weekly recommendations or monthly recommendations), percentage allocation to each recommendation, investment start date, investment stop date (blank means continue with investment) - should be configurable by the user.
The user should be able to see the returns of both strategies in a chart or table. They should be able to export this data as a CSV file.
The user should also be able to store these scenarios and compare them later.
